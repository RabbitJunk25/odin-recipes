Lasagne

Description

One hour from start-to-finish (and half of that's in the oven) and you can be serving up a big meaty-cheesy dish of lasagne, with enough for second helpings. Because we all want second helpings when it comes to lasagne right?

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Lasagne sheets
  • Pasta sauce
  • salt and pepper
  • White sauce
  • Beef ragu

Steps

  1. We start by frying onion in a pan with a little oil. Add garlic, carrot and finely chopped mushrooms (this bulks out the dish and adds flavour).
  2. Slowly stir in milk, and keep stirring (using a hand whisk) over the heat until thickened. Stir in cheese and a pinch of salt and pepper and turn off the heat.
  3. Now take your lasagne dish (mine is a rectangular enamel roaster, approx 31cmx25cm and 5cm deep.
  4. Spread a little of the meat sauce over the base of the dish. You don't need a lot. It's just to stop the lasagne sheets from sticking to the base of the dish.
  5. Top with lasagne sheets (try not to overlap, but we don't want big spaces either). Then top with a third of the b√©chamel, followed by a third of the meat sauce.
  6. Then sprinkle on the cheese and place in a preheated oven at 200C/400F for 25 - 30 minutes, until the cheese is golden.
