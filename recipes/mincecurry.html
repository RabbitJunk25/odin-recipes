Mince Curry

Description

This is a traditional mince curry dish, made with beef mince and a hanful of indian spices to transform ordinary beef mince into a delicious family dinner packed with great flavour. Serve with rice, pap or mash, or pile your mince on hot buttered toast.

Ingredients

  • 1KG Beef mince
  • 1 Diced tomato
  • 1 Potato cut into 6 pieces
  • 1 Diced onion
  • Chilli powder
  • Kashmiri paste
  • 2 Bayleaf
  • 2 tbs ginger garlic paste
  • salt

Steps

  1. put 1 tbs canola oil in heated pot
  2. add bayleaf , aniseed
  3. add diced onion and cook till light brown
  4. add diced tomato and stir
  5. add all spices and half cup water and mix well
  6. add cut potato and mix well and let pot simmer
  7. add lean beef mince and mix
  8. add salt to taste
  9. leave on hot stove for 15 - 20 minutes
